Privacy

ADAMZ processes uploaded datasets to generate cleaning and analytics insights. Files are used only for requested analysis workflows.

What we collect

  • Uploaded tabular data files and related export artifacts you request.
  • Transaction metadata (payment confirmation IDs, timestamps, file names) for audit and support.
  • Basic technical metadata (browser type, request logs, and error traces) for service reliability.

How we use data

  • To clean, analyze, and visualize your dataset in dashboard form.
  • To generate downloadable outputs such as cleaned CSV/XLSX and dashboard PDF files.
  • To detect abuse, troubleshoot failures, and improve platform performance.

Data retention

  • Payment and transaction logs are retained for operational and accounting purposes.
  • Stored artifacts are retained based on your deployment storage policy and provider configuration.
  • You can request deletion support for specific transaction artifacts when needed.

Your responsibilities

  • Avoid uploading sensitive personal data unless your compliance policies explicitly allow it.
  • Mask or pseudonymize regulated fields before upload whenever possible.
  • Ensure you have legal rights to process and analyze the uploaded datasets.
