Security

ADAMZ applies practical controls to protect your data workflows and account operations.

Controls

  • HTTPS transport for data in transit.
  • Access controls and separation of payment flow and analytics flow.
  • Regular dependency updates and vulnerability patching.

Recommendations

  • Mask sensitive identifiers before upload where possible.
  • Use internal governance checks for regulated datasets.
